Message from our Director

I am thrilled to welcome you to Arise Technical Training Institute (ATTI), where we embark on a transformative journey of knowledge and skill acquisition. As the Principal Director, it brings me immense joy to witness the inception of this institute, dedicated to providing cutting-edge technical education.
At ATTI, our vision is to empower individuals with the skills and expertise needed to thrive in today's dynamic and technology-driven world. We understand the pivotal role that technical training plays in shaping future leaders, innovators, and problem-solvers. Our commitment is to deliver high-quality, hands-on training that goes beyond traditional education, ensuring our learners are well-prepared for the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.
Our dedicated team of experienced instructors and staff is driven by a passion for education and a belief in the potential of every learner. We aim to create a learning environment that fosters curiosity, critical thinking, and collaboration. As you embark on your educational journey with ATTI, I encourage you to embrace challenges, seek knowledge, and aspire to make a meaningful impact in your chosen field.

Warm regards,
Dr. Geoffery Mariga, PHD
Founder, Arise Technical Training Institute (ATTI)
